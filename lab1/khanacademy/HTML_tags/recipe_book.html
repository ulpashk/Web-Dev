Recipe #2

Chicken & Biscuit Bake

  • Time: 1 h
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
onion 1
flour 60 g
chicken broth 3 cups
salt 1 teaspoon
heavy cream 240 mL
chicken 1 250 g
biscuit doug 2 tubes

Step 1: In a large pot over medium-high heat, melt the butter. Add the onion, stir until softened. About 1 minute. Whisk in the flour, stirring constantly to prevent the flour from browning. About 1 minute.

Step 2: Whisk in the chicken broth, salt, and pepper. Continue to whisk until no lumps remain. Whisk in the heavy cream. Bring to a light simmer until the sauce has thickened slightly. Test for additional salt/pepper.

Step 3: Add the mixed vegetables and shredded chicken, stir until fully incorporated. Evenly top with 12 pieces of biscuit dough (you will have extra biscuits, which can be baked off separately). Bake in a preheated oven for 20 minutes, until the biscuits are golden and the gravy mixture is bubbling.

Step 4: Enjoy!

Recipe #3

Butter Chicken

  • Time: 20-25 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
chicken breast 910 g
unsalted butter 6 tablespoons
yellow onion 225 g
ground cumin 1 teaspoon
garlic 3 cloves
tomato sauce 395 g

Step 1: In a large bowl, season the chicken with salt, pepper, 1 teaspoon of chili powder, and the turmeric. Let sit for 15 minutes to marinate.

Step 2: Melt another 2 tablespoons of butter in the pot, then add the onion, garam masala, remaining teaspoon of chili powder, the cumin, ginger, garlic, cayenne, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Cook until fragrant.

Step 3: Return the chicken to the pot, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Stir in the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and season with more salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Serve the chicken over rice and garnish with cilantro. Enjoy!
